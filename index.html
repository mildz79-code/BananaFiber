Green Whisper® Technology

From Harvest
Waste to
Premium Fiber

Banana trees produce fruit only once in their lifetime — after each harvest, the trunk is cut down. Worldwide, this creates billions of tons of agricultural waste, often burned or dumped illegally.

Green Whisper® developed proprietary field-extraction technology based in Coignières, France that transforms this waste directly at the farm into textile-grade fiber coils — blended with cotton for superior performance.

Material Performance

Why Banana Fiber?

A fiber that outperforms convention — stronger, more sustainable, and built for longevity.

High Tear Resistance

The banana-cotton blend yields greater tear resistance than standard cotton, extending garment lifespan under regular wear.

Low-Temp Washable

Engineered for care at reduced temperatures — saving energy through every wash cycle without compromise.

Longer Lifespan

Structural properties ensure the garment maintains form and color longer than conventional cotton tees.

100% Biodegradable

No synthetics in the fiber blend. The end-of-life garment fully biodegrades, closing the loop on a circular model.

Zero New Farmland

Raw material is pure agricultural residue. The banana plant is harvested for fruit first — fiber is a pure byproduct.

Vertically Produced

One Textile manages the full chain — fiber to knitting, dyeing at Color Fashion, cut-&-sew — quality at every step.
